Lexa – AI-Powered Dyslexia Detection

Revolutionizing early dyslexia screening: Lexa is a mobile application that empowers parents and teachers to detect potential dyslexia in children aged 5–10 through AI analysis of reading, writing, and speaking behaviors captured in short video clips.

Project Overview

Developed with real-world data from special schools in Narowal, Punjab, Lexa provides accessible early screening to bridge the gap between initial concerns and professional diagnosis. The app makes dyslexia detection possible anywhere, reducing barriers to early intervention.

Dataset Composition

  • 400 carefully annotated videos (200 dyslexic, 200 non-dyslexic children)
  • Age range: 5–10 years (critical window for early intervention)
  • Activity types: Reading aloud, writing samples, verbal responses
  • Data augmentation: Applied variations in lighting, angles, and background noise
  • Ethical collection: Partnered with special education schools in Punjab, Pakistan
